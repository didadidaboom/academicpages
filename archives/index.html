About me

Over the last half year, Wu Chong has shifted his focus to his interest areas of data analysis, AI algorithms, and recommender systems without hurrying up to work and also exploring the internet area in view of a full stack developer. To reach these goals, he accessed some open projects (黑马头条, etc.), which primarily relate to frameworks, such as Hadoop, Hive, Spark, SQL, PyTorch, etc. Meanwhile, he also started his own project “BKMIST“, a social community released as a mini-program in Wechat, to let himself experience how a full-stack developer should do and think. His connection with the internet area goes checking more here.

He made his decision to withdraw from Nanyang Technological University (NTU) at the end of the year 2021. Before that, he passed QE (qualifying examination) and was named as a Ph.D. candidate, supervised by Asst Prof Chan Wai Lee, working on the Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) that requires the abilities to acquire programming languages quickly and to explore different open source frameworks. He obtained his degree of M.E. in 2018 from the School of Energy and Power Engineering at Beihang University with his work in the CFD area. He received his degree of B.E. from Nanchang Hangkong University in 2014.
